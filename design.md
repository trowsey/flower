## Characters

The four playable classes are gameplay-archetype mappings of the design:
agile/melee, heavy/melee, ranged/magic, and balanced/support.

### Sarah — Bladedancer (agile melee)
- 21, Mexico City
- Green hair, long past her butt; purple eyes; olive skin
- Wears a blue dress, yellow pants, orange shoes with black laces
- Long, skinny, very sharp sword
- Fast agile fighter — high attack speed, lower health, high crit
- Class identity: **Rogue/Bladedancer** — risk/reward dodger

### Maddie — Bruiser (heavy melee)
- 29, Dublin
- Yellow eyes; golden hair, short like a bob; peach skin
- Red crop top, red shorts, red knee-high boots
- Pounds her enemies with hard punches — slow, hard, high damage
- Has a cousin (TBD: future co-op flavor / quest hook)
- Class identity: **Barbarian/Bruiser** — high health, slow swings, knockback

### Chan Xaic — Soulcaster (ranged magic)
- 26, Beijing
- Black hair tied back; calm demeanor
- Robe-like silk garments in deep red and gold trim
- Channels demon-essence into ranged soul-bolts and area curses
- Lower health and move speed; highest soul/mana pool
- Class identity: **Sorcerer/Soulcaster** — ranged kiter, AOE specialist

### Aiyana — Wardweaver (balanced/support)
- 24, Tucson (Tohono O'odham)
- Dark hair in two long braids; warm brown eyes
- Earth-toned leathers with turquoise accents; wields a curved short blade and a small ward-shield
- Toggles between melee strikes and protective wards that shield allies and slow enemies
- Balanced stats; the natural "anchor" in 2-player co-op
- Class identity: **Paladin/Wardweaver** — moderate everything, party utility

Each character has unique base stats, a distinct starter weapon, and a
signature skill. See `scripts/items/character_class.gd` for stat values.
